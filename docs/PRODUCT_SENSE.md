# Product Sense

## Product

Static marketing site for Luan Alves, focused on selling web development services to businesses in Brazil.

## Primary Audience

- Small and medium businesses that need a professional website.
- Service businesses that rely on WhatsApp leads.
- Businesses comparing site institucional, landing page, blog profissional, and broader criação de sites work.
- Local-intent visitors looking for criação de sites em Belém.

## Primary Conversion

WhatsApp contact. CTAs should make the next step clear and specific.

## Positioning

Luan Alves is positioned as a freelance web developer who builds fast, professional websites with technical SEO and conversion focus.

## Current Public Surface

- Service pages for criação de sites, site institucional, landing page, blog profissional, and criação de sites em Belém.
- Portfolio index and individual case pages.
- Insights index and individual editorial articles.
- Public `llms.txt` summarizing canonical pages, cases, articles, business facts, and contact.

## Product Principles

- Clarity before decoration.
- Performance and SEO are selling points, not hidden implementation details.
- Real portfolio proof should support service claims.
- Editorial content should route readers toward service pages, cases, and WhatsApp.
- Copy should feel direct, professional, and local to `pt-BR`.

## Non-Goals

- Generic SaaS landing-page style.
- Blog content with no commercial path.
- Decorative visuals that hide the real work.
- Adding backend complexity without a clear product need.
