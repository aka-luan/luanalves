# Main Flows

## Service Lead Flow

1. Visitor lands on home or a service page.
2. Visitor reads service fit, proof, process, FAQ, and related portfolio/examples.
3. Visitor clicks a WhatsApp CTA.
4. Conversation continues outside the site.

## Portfolio Proof Flow

1. Visitor opens `/portfolio/` or a featured portfolio card.
2. Visitor filters or selects a case.
3. Visitor reads challenge, solution, deliverables, result, gallery, and related links.
4. Visitor clicks service or WhatsApp CTA.

## Editorial Flow

1. Visitor opens `/insights/` or an article URL.
2. Visitor reads practical guidance.
3. Article links route visitor toward a service page, portfolio proof, or WhatsApp.

## Local SEO Flow

1. Visitor searches with Belém/local intent.
2. Visitor lands on `/criacao-de-sites-belem/`.
3. Page explains local relevance and service value.
4. Visitor contacts through WhatsApp.
